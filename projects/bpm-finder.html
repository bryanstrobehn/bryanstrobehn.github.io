how to use this
  1. Separate your stems first — use Moises or Fadr (both free tiers available) to split your track into bass, melody, drums, and other.
  2. Drop each stem into the slots below. You don’t need all four — drop what you have.
  3. Hit analyze — each stem gets independently analyzed for pitch, key, timbre, and spectral character.
  4. Read the report — use it as a starting brief for recreation in Ableton. Not perfect, but faster than pure ear transcription.
