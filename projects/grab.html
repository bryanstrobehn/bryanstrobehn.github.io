Grab

Built out of a genuine moment of friction — needed to quickly show something on screen to friends in Signal and realized there was no tool that was just: open, click, done.

No account. No upload. No cloud. The recording stays on your machine and downloads as a .webm file you can drag straight into Slack, Signal, Discord, or wherever.

On mobile, screen capture isn't available — but camera recording works the same way. Same one-click flow, file lands in your downloads.
